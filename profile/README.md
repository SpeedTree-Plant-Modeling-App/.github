## 🚀 At a Glance
SpeedTree for Windows is a focused tool for modeling 3D plants and trees, from individual specimens to full forests of foliage. It blends procedural generation with precise manual editing, letting you define how a plant grows and then refine the branches, leaves, and silhouette. Widely adopted in game development and used across film and visualization work, SpeedTree produces vegetation that carries cleanly into the engines and renderers teams already rely on.

1. Open SpeedTree and create a new model or load a plant from the library.
